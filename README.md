# InnoSage AI Newsletter System

This repository hosts all resources, implementations, configurations, and AI news sources for the InnoSage AI Newsletter system. It is a strategic corporate asset designed to showcase our "Performance-First" architecture and automate our technical marketing.

## Strategic Focus (Q1 2026)

- **Target Persona:** Engineering Teams and Technical Freelancers.
- **Core Wedge:** Sub-100ms latency via local-first architecture (leveraging our Notion-style Markdown editor).
- **Key Differentiator:** "Ephemeral Security" (Burn After Reading) for secure sharing and "Custom Domain Publishing."

## Repository Structure

- **/sources**: Curated lists of AI news sources (RSS, Substack, X).
- **/engine**: Scripts for fetching, summarizing, and formatting news via LLMs.
- **/templates**: Markdown-based newsletter templates (e.g., "Stable Output").
- **/publishing**: Configurations for Cloudflare Workers and R2 storage.
- **/archives**: History of all published newsletters in Markdown format.
- **/.agent**: Custom instructions for AI agents to assist with curation and editing.

## "Stable Output" (情绪稳定输出端)

Our editorial persona focuses on providing calm, rational, and high-signal updates to cut through the industry's "AI Hype." We prioritize quality of insight over frequency of posting.
